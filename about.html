About Me

As a web design student, I'm passionate about transforming ideas into clean, user-centered digital experiences. In August, I will graduate with an Associate of Applied Science in Digital Media degree with a focus on web design. Having earned a Web Design Certificate from Northwest Vista College, I am now working toward a Bachelor of Applied Technology in Cloud Computing. This course of study has provided a comprehensive understanding of Adobe XD, Bootstrap, CSS, Figma, GitHub, HTML, JavaScript, SASS, Visual Studio Code, and WordPress.

Creativity, dependability, and a thirst for knowledge are among my strongest assets. People often say I have a positive attitude and a welcoming presence, which helps me connect with others, especially in team settings. My objective is to secure a web design internship. This is an opportunity to use my skills to build functional and visually engaging projects that make a real-world difference.

Every website has a story to tell, and my mission is to help clients share their unique stories with the world.
